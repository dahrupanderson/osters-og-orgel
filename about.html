Öisters & Orgel


At oyster and orgel we are putting your oyster experience as our main priority! We are proud to present some of the finest selection of oyster from north of france, Copenhagen has to offer.

As a child the owner of Öister & Orgel, Hugo Bernard, would come home from school to his grandmothers house and eat supper. His grandfather was a great fisherman and would often bring home oysters for dinner. So while he would eat, his grandmother would play the small house organ in the living room. And so the concept for Öister & Orgel was born.

Hugo moved to Copenhagen in 2010, and brought his French heritage along with him. In 2016 Öister & Orgel opened at Vesterbro, and you are now able to experience the finest dinning experince alongside the musicalscore of Hugo's childhood every friday.

Where do you find us?

Öister & Orgel opened in 2016 at Vesterbro in a lovely old warehouse near the meatpack district. Find us at Halmtorvet 303a, 1786 Vesterbro
